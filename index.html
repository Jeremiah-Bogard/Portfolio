At first I was just doing it to count as school, then I got the hang of it and decided that it was the coolest thing ever. How I Taught Myself

Like most people I learned through YouTube. The most useful person I found through that goes by the YouTube name of Web Dev Simplified. I would recommend him to every Web Developer I know. However, I did not just use YouTube to learn. I also took free online courses provided by individuals and companies. The most common ones being Free Code Camp and Khan Academy.
